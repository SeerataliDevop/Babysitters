F A Q's

Service Information

  • What services are you providing?

    •  Regular cleaning,Sanitization cleaning, Disinfection services, Babysitting, Ironing


  • Can I ask my helper to do multiple tasks?

    •  Yes, you may ask the helper to do as many chores and tasks within the duration of the booking. For example, if you book a cleaner who is also a cook, then you ask him/her to do both cleaning and cooking as long as it falls within the duration of the booking.


  • What are your working hours and days?

    •  We operate from 10am to 6pm, Monday through Saturday.


  • Are the helpers supposed to get a break and if yes, for how long?

    •  We strongly recommend a break of 15 minutes after the first 2 hours of work. This will ensure that our helpers can continue to work effectively and serve you well. The total booking time is inclusive of these break times. For example, a 3-hour booking includes the 15-minute break you should allow the helper to take after 2 hours of work.
